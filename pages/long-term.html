Long-term Vision

The long term vison has a few components

Branch Growth

This phase will roll out upon or alongside the plans set out in Phase 2 (Medium Term vision)
The idea is to deploy similar models to various countries in the African continent. There is no limitation to this scope, any other countries are welcome to join in

DeGov

The DeGov phase is a method of striving for Decentralzed Governance, part of the Voltaire phase of the Cardano roadmap
This is set to be the most difficult part on the part of IOHK and we will follow the updates eagerly
From my inital thoughts, there will need to be a digital constitution that is composed by the community and pins values of the Cardano vision
This phase could happen in a few ways: a constitutional alteration inside an existing country or alongside it
This is very much in the distant future and Africapools hopes to set down the essential groundwork in Africa in order for Voltaire to have fertile soil to grow in
