Medium-term Vision

once a viable financial model has been achieved as described in Stage 1
a model would need to be developed for the deployment of similar systems into other African Countries (branch stake pools)
The development of sibling stake pools would require for independant SPOs to be:

  • Found
  • Trained
  • Certified
  • Audited regularly

These branch stake pools will operate independantly but in a similar model to the original, with a commitment to:
  1. Decentralization
  2. Stability and Security
  3. Local adoption and charity
During the inital setup + auditing process, the stake rewards of branch Stake pools for an initially determined period will go into the development of AfricaPools as an organization.
These stake rewards will begin to revert back to the branch stake pool upon passing an Audit and then incrementally as POC charity initiatives are demonstrated.
