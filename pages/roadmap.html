Roadmap

The Idea

Welcome to Africapools.io. This project is intended to be a starting point for the future adoption of Cardano in Africa
The short term vision is to develop the skills and tools to run a charity Stake pool that is financially viable to fund itself
The medium term vision is to develop training, auditing and certification to Stake Pool Operators, specifically in Africa.
The long term vision is to have a Cardano Stake pool in every country in Africa, starting with those countries listed in the 2Africa project
